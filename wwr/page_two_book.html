What can you do on book-worm

What questions will be answered?

On ‘book-worm’ visitors will be able to find a plethora of answers to their questions. For example, you will be able to find detailed book recommendations. This may include but is not limited to book recommendations for sex, drugs, language, and innuendo content in books. With this information, readers will be able to determine if the books they want to read are appropriate for them. While there are other sites that do the same thing, ‘book-worm will be precise and will have ratings from other websites and people for visitors to look at to provide multiple sources in one location. Additionally, on 'book-worm' people will be able to find lots of book recommendations!

What can you do on book-worm?

While ‘book-worm’ is a website for one to check book recommendations, the main purpose of the site is for readers to track and rate the books they have read. With the assistance of the recommendations found in ‘book-worm,’ readers will be able to be confident in their book choices and will not hesitate to read books. Some of the activities visitors will be able to do will be: rate books, sort books into different piles ( like a to-read pile, read pile… ), create clubs, and track their reading goals.In order to provide all of these activities on 'book-worm', we need the viewer's input. When viewers input their ratings, favorite and least favorite books, 'book-worm' will be able to provide more activities for people to participate in and enjoy.
