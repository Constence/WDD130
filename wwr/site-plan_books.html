Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been recommended a book by a friend, but once you start reading it, you notice that it has things that go against your standards? With my website, you will be able to look at ratings, warnings, and purchase edited books so this will never happen again. With my website, customers will not only be able to keep a personal record of the books they have read, but they will also be able to leave reviews about warnings about content for fellow consumers! Additionally, there will be generated warnings like age ratings, sex-related content, and language usage from the internet all in one location! My goal is to make sure readers of all ages can enjoy books to the fullest without the worry of uncomfortable content, by buying edited books or by looking at the recommendations.

Audience

My intended audience would be 12 and up, male and female. The content of books, or misleading books, have become a large problem for people of all ages. Whether it is because someone is trying to find a book for their own personal use or trying to inspect a book for a friend or a loved one. The lack of websites that can provide accurate warnings for books is astonishing. However, with ‘Book-Worm’, people will be able to find a large variety of reviews and ratings in one website

Home page

What is book-worm?

Many people will want to use ‘bookworm’ because it will provide a safe area for people to look up book recommendations. Many bookworms would agree that when you are enjoying a book it is the worst to put it away because it makes you feel uneasy in some shape, way, or form. With ‘book-worm’ readers will be able to distinguish what books are appropriate for them without having to end a book halfway through. Additionally, not only will there be content warnings generated and provided by other viewers, but on 'book-worm' you will be able to keep a record of your personal reading history. In 'book-worm' you will be able to list, organize and rate all of the books you have read or plan on reading.

Why visit book-worm

People will come to ‘book-worm’ rather than another site because we have everything located in one site. On ‘book-worm’ you will be able to have all of the recommendations, warnings, personal records on one site. While there are other book-related sites out there, few have everything a book lover needs in one location. Not only will there be countless accessible actions for views to work on, but multiple questions can also be answered all in one website.

What can you do on book-worm?

While ‘book-worm’ is a website for one to check book recommendations, the main purpose of the site is for readers to track and rate the books they have read. With the assistance of the recommendations found in ‘book-worm,’ readers will be able to be confident in their book choices and will not hesitate to read books. Some of the activities visitors will be able to do will be: rate books, sort books into different piles ( like a to-read pile, read pile… ), submit a video recording of ratings, host zooms for book clubs, create clubs, create 'personal genres', and track their reading goals. With all of these activities for viewers will not have to have 4 websites open to do the same things. In order to provide all of these activities on 'book-worm', we need the viewer's input. When viewers input their ratings, favorite and least favorite books, 'book-worm' will be able to provide more activities for people to participate in and enjoy.

Page three: Social media

Book-wrom is an social media! While 'book-worm' is an informational site, it is also a form of social media. On 'book-worm' one will be able to post videos and other content for others to see and read. Not only will you be able to post content, but you will also be able to gain a following! With this, you will be able to find others who have the same taste in books, and help you find more books to add to your 'to read pile'. As you find more friends on 'book-worm' you will be able to form virtual book clubs and find recommendations from friends!
