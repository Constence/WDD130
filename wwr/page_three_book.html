Track your reading

Book-wrom is an social media! While 'book-worm' is an informational site, it is also a form of social media. On 'book-worm' one will be able to post videos and other content for others to see and read. Not only will you be able to post content, but you will also be able to gain a following! With this, you will be able to find others who have the same taste in books, and help you find more books to add to your 'to read pile'. As you find more friends on 'book-worm' you will be able to form virtual book clubs and find recommendations from friends!
