Book-Worm

Track your reading

While ‘book-worm’ is a website for one to check book recommendations, the main purpose of the site is for readers to track and rate the books they have read. With the assistance of the recommendations found in ‘book-worm,’ readers will be able to be confident in their book choices and will not hesitate to read books. Some of the activities visitors will be able to do will be: rate books, sort books into different piles ( like a to-read pile, read pile… ), submit a video recording of ratings, host zooms for book clubs, create clubs, create 'personal genres', and track their reading goals. With all of these activities for viewers will not have to have 4 websites open to do the same things. In order to provide all of these activities on 'book-worm', we need the viewer's input. When viewers input their ratings, favorite and least favorite books, 'book-worm' will be able to provide more activities for people to participate in and enjoy.
